📰 Newspaper Columns

Strips an article to pure text and renders it in full-screen columns. Space or arrow keys turn the page — no scrolling. Adjust font, size, columns, and theme on the fly.

Drag this button to your bookmarks bar:

📰 Newspaper Columns

Can't drag? See instructions below.

How to install

  1. Make sure your bookmarks bar is visible in Chrome: ⌘ Shift B
  2. Drag the black button above directly onto your bookmarks bar.
  3. Done. Navigate to any article and click 📰 Newspaper Columns in your bar.

Can't drag? Add it manually

  1. Right-click your bookmarks bar → Add page…
  2. Name: 📰 Newspaper Columns
  3. URL: paste the code from the box below (click to select all):
Turn pages: Space / → advance · ← back · Esc close.
Adjust: F font · −/+ size · C columns (auto/1–4) · B theme (Cream/White/Sepia/Night).
Your choices are remembered (per site, via localStorage). Reaching the last page closes the reader automatically.

What it strips: images, figures, video, ads, sidebars, nav, forms, cookie banners, comments.
