📰 Newspaper Columns

Strips an article to pure text and renders it in 3 full-screen columns. Space or arrow keys turn the page — no scrolling.

Drag this button to your bookmarks bar:

📰 Newspaper Columns

Can't drag? See instructions below.

How to install

  1. Make sure your bookmarks bar is visible in Chrome: ⌘ Shift B
  2. Drag the black button above directly onto your bookmarks bar.
  3. Done. Navigate to any article and click 📰 Newspaper Columns in your bar.

Can't drag? Add it manually

  1. Right-click your bookmarks bar → Add page…
  2. Name: 📰 Newspaper Columns

Controls: Space or → to advance · ← to go back · ESC or ✕ to close.
Reaching the last page closes the reader automatically.

What it strips: images, figures, video, ads, sidebars, nav, forms, cookie banners, comments.
